Same Day Car Insurance for Immigrants: Get Instant Proof of Coverage Today

If you need insurance today and you are worried your immigration status will cause problems, it will not. Insurance companies are not immigration enforcement and cannot report you to ICE.

🇺🇸 All 50 States All Immigration Statuses 🚗 Getting Insured
Quick Answer: Same day car insurance means you can buy a policy and receive valid proof of insurance within hours or minutes. Most major insurers issue digital ID cards instantly after purchase. No SSN is required by law to buy car insurance.
Important: Driving without insurance is illegal in nearly every US state and exposes you to fines, lawsuits, and vehicle impoundment — get covered before you drive.
💡 After you pay for your policy, save your digital insurance card to your phone immediately so you have proof of coverage ready if you are stopped while driving.

Can You Get Car Insurance the Same Day as an Immigrant?

Yes — most major insurers issue digital proof of insurance instantly after you pay, even if you are an immigrant without a Social Security Number.

You do not need to be a citizen or permanent resident to buy car insurance in the United States. Coverage is available to you regardless of immigration status.

Many immigrants get insured online in under 30 minutes. You will receive a digital insurance card by email that is legally valid the same day.

What Documents Do You Need Ready Before You Apply?

Have your driver license ready. This can be a foreign license, an international driving permit, or a state license issued under laws like California AB60.

Over 19 states allow undocumented immigrants to obtain a driver license. Check the full list at NCSL.org.

If you do not have an SSN, use your ITIN or passport number. Many insurers accept these as identification. Learn about ITINs at IRS.gov.

Also have your vehicle registration, the VIN number of your car, and a payment method ready before starting your quote.

Which Insurers Issue Instant Proof of Insurance?

Progressive, GEICO, State Farm, and many regional insurers allow you to buy a policy online and download proof of insurance within minutes of payment.

Some insurers specialize in serving immigrants and accept foreign licenses or ITINs without issue. Rates vary by insurer and state — always get multiple quotes.

Avoid insurers that require an SSN as the only identification option. Many alternatives exist. An independent insurance agent can help you find immigrant-friendly options fast.

The National Association of Insurance Commissioners provides a directory to verify that any insurer you choose is licensed in your state.

Will Buying Insurance Put Your Immigration Status at Risk?

No. Insurance companies are not immigration enforcement agencies and do not report customers to ICE. Your privacy is legally protected.

The Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act requires insurers to protect your personal data. Read more at the FTC official site.

Buying insurance does not create an immigration record. It is a private financial transaction between you and a regulated company.

Driving without insurance is the actual risk. It can result in fines, license suspension, or vehicle impoundment — all of which create far more legal exposure than buying a policy.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • 1

    Gather your documents first

    Collect your foreign license, passport, ITIN or matricula consular, and vehicle title. You do not need an SSN to get insured today.

  • 2

    Find insurers that accept immigrants

    Contact insurers known to work with non-citizen drivers. Many accept foreign licenses and ITIN numbers. Rates vary, so get multiple quotes.

  • 3

    Request same-day or instant quotes

    Call or apply online and ask specifically for same-day coverage. Many insurers can bind a policy within hours of your application.

  • 4

    Choose your coverage level

    Select at minimum your state required liability coverage. Adding comprehensive and collision protects your vehicle. Requirements vary by state.

  • 5

    Pay and receive your proof of insurance

    Pay your first premium to activate the policy. Your insurer will email or text proof of insurance immediately. Save it on your phone.

  • 6

    Understand your privacy is protected

    Insurers are not immigration enforcement. The Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act protects your data. Buying insurance does not alert ICE.

  • 7

    Keep your documents accessible

    Store digital and printed copies of your insurance card, registration, and license together. You will need all three if stopped while driving.
