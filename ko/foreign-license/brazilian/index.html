Can you drive with a Brazilian license in the US?

Yes, you can drive with a valid Brazilian driver license in most US states for short visits. However, if you are staying permanently or plan to work, you will need a US state driver license or ID.

Many Brazilian immigrants in Florida and Massachusetts worry about getting a US license. The good news: you do not need an SSN or immigration status to get one in many states.

Check your state DMV website for exact requirements. Some states accept foreign licenses for up to one year; others require you to get a state license within 30 days of establishing residency.

Do insurance companies accept Brazilian licenses?

Yes. Most major US insurers will quote and sell you a policy using a valid Brazilian driver license. You do not need a US license to buy car insurance right now.

However, insurers may charge higher rates or ask extra questions if your only ID is foreign. Getting a US driver license (even without SSN) usually lowers your rate and makes the process faster.

Call insurers directly and ask: "I have a Brazilian license. Can I get a quote?" Do not feel embarrassed. Immigrant drivers are normal customers.

What ID do you need to buy insurance as a Brazilian immigrant?

You can use your Brazilian passport or Brazilian driver license as primary ID. If you have an ITIN (Individual Taxpayer Identification Number), that helps but is not required for insurance.

Get an ITIN from the IRS even if you do not work yet. It opens doors for insurance, bank accounts, and future credit. Learn more at IRS ITIN page.

Insurance companies are NOT immigration enforcement. Buying insurance does not report you to ICE or immigration authorities. Your insurance data is protected by federal privacy law.

How to get a US driver license in Florida or Massachusetts without SSN

Florida and Massachusetts both allow you to get a driver license using an ITIN instead of SSN. Visit your state DMV, bring your Brazilian passport, proof of residence (lease or utility bill), and ITIN documentation.

Massachusetts: Go to your local RMV office with original Brazilian license, passport, and proof of address. The process takes 2-4 weeks.

Florida: Visit a local DMV with the same documents. You may take the written test in Portuguese if needed. A US license will lower your insurance quotes immediately and remove barriers to employment.
