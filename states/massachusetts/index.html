Car Insurance for Immigrants in Massachusetts

Massachusetts is one of the few states that allows undocumented immigrants to get a standard driver license. Regardless of your immigration status or documentation, you can legally buy car insurance in Massachusetts.

🇺🇸 All 50 States All Immigration Statuses 🚗 Car Insurance
Quick Answer: Massachusetts requires all drivers to carry auto insurance, but you do not need an SSN or US driver license to buy it. You can use an ITIN, foreign license, or state ID. Many insurers in Massachusetts work with immigrants and will not report you to immigration authorities.
Important: Massachusetts RMV requires a Social Security number or ITIN to get a driver license—undocumented immigrants without an ITIN cannot currently obtain one.
💡 If you have an ITIN from the IRS, you can use it to apply for both a Massachusetts driver license and car insurance.

Massachusetts Minimum Car Insurance Requirements

Massachusetts requires all drivers to carry liability insurance with minimum limits of $20,000 for bodily injury per person, $40,000 total per accident, and $5,000 for property damage.

You must have proof of insurance in your vehicle at all times. Driving without insurance can result in fines up to $500, license suspension, and registration cancellation.

Massachusetts also requires uninsured motorist coverage as part of your policy.

Can Immigrants Get a License in Massachusetts?

Massachusetts does NOT currently allow undocumented immigrants to get a standard driver license. You need a valid Social Security number or ITIN to apply.

If you have an ITIN, you can use it to qualify for a license. Visit your local RMV (Registry of Motor Vehicles) with proof of identity, residency, and your ITIN number.

Check the NCSL database for states that do allow licenses regardless of status: https://www.ncsl.org/transportation/states-that-allow-unauthorized-immigrants-to-get-a-drivers-license

Best Insurance Options for Immigrants in Massachusetts

Many national insurers accept ITIN numbers instead of SSN in Massachusetts, including Geico, Progressive, State Farm, and Allstate. You must have a valid driver license or ID to get a quote.

If you have a valid MA license, you qualify for standard rates. If you have a foreign license, some insurers will still quote you—rates vary, get a quote from multiple companies.

Never lie about your status or documents. Insurance companies are not immigration enforcement and your data is protected by the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act.

Tips for Immigrant Drivers in Massachusetts

Massachusetts has strict drunk driving laws and mandatory seat belt laws. Always carry proof of insurance and your license.

The RMV offers license renewal online if you have a standard license. In-person visits may require an appointment—check the MA RMV website first.

If you are stopped by police, you have the right to remain silent and ask for a lawyer. Buying insurance does NOT alert ICE or any immigration agency.
