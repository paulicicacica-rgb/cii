Can I use my Brazilian driver license to buy car insurance in the US?

Yes, many US insurers accept a valid Brazilian driver license as proof of driving experience. You do not need a US license to get insured, though you will need to be a US resident or have a US address.

Florida and Massachusetts both allow foreign licenses for insurance purposes. Check with your insurer first, as some companies have different rules about which countries they accept licenses from.

Having a Brazilian license actually helps you get coverage faster because it proves you have driving experience. Insurance companies see this as lower risk than someone with no driving history.

Do I need an ITIN, SSN, or visa to get car insurance with a Brazilian license?

No. You do not need a Social Security Number or visa to buy car insurance. An ITIN (Individual Taxpayer Identification Number) makes it easier, but is not required by insurers.

Most insurers will ask for your name, address, license number, and driving history. You can provide this information even without an ITIN. Get an ITIN from the IRS if you plan to file taxes: IRS ITIN page.

Buying insurance does not report you to immigration. Insurance companies are not law enforcement. Your insurance information is protected by privacy law.

What if I want to get a US or state driver license instead?

Florida and Massachusetts both allow you to get a state license using your Brazilian driver license and proof of residency. This makes insurance even easier to obtain and cheaper to maintain.

Massachusetts accepts foreign licenses for exchange. Florida does not have a direct exchange, but you can take the written and road tests with your Brazilian license as proof of experience.

A US state license will lower your insurance rates because insurers trust state-issued ID more than foreign licenses. Ask your DMV about the process in your state.

How do I get a quote quickly with my Brazilian license?

Call or visit websites of insurers that serve your state and say you have a foreign license. Have ready: your Brazilian license number, US address, and your driving history from Brazil if available.

Florida has many insurers experienced with Brazilian immigrants. Massachusetts has fewer, so call ahead. Getting a quote takes 5 to 10 minutes and does not commit you to anything.

Be honest about your driving record. If you have accidents or tickets from Brazil, tell the insurer. They may still cover you. Rates vary based on your record, age, and vehicle—get multiple quotes to compare.
